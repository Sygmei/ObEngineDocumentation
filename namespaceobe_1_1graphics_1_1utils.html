Draws a Polygon.

Parameters
surfaceRenderSurface where to render the polygon
pointsList of all the Polygon points positions
optionsDrawing options :
  • <lines> bool : Are the lines of the Polygon visible ? (true = visible, false = not visible)
  • <points> bool : Are the points of the Polygon visible ? (true = visible, false = not visible)
  • <radius> unsigned int : The radius of the points of the Polygon.
  • <line_color> Color : Default color of the lines of the Polygon.
  • <line_color_x> Color : Color of a specific line of the Polygon where x is the index of the line
  • <point_color> Color : Default color of the points of the Polygon
  • <point_color_x> Color : Color of a specific point of the Polygon where x is the index of the point
