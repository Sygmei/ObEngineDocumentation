◆ AnimationPlayMode

enum obe::Animation::AnimationPlayMode
strong

The Play Mode of an Animation.
It indicates whether an Animation can be interrupted or not and what to do when the Animation is over.

Enumerator
OneTime 

The Animation will play once and stay at last texture.

Loop 

The Animation will play in loop and can be interrupted at any time.

Force 

The Animation will be played Once and can't be interrupted.

◆ AnimationStatus

enum obe::Animation::AnimationStatus
strong

The AnimationStatus indicates whether the current Animation should continue to play or call another one.

Enumerator
Play 

The Animation continues to play.

Call 

The Animation will call another one.
The name of the Animation to call is stored in m_animationToCall.
You can also get the Animation name to call by using Animation::getCalledAnimation().
